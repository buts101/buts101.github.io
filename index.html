Background

Nepal Telecom has procured Huawei under Contract No. NDCL/BTD/05/072-73 IP/MPLS Optical Transmission Equipment for Spur Network and; CX600 series routers and ATN910C series routers under Contract No. NDCL/BTD/05/075-76 IPRAN Equipment. Most of CX600 routers are used as aggregation routers in all over the country. Some of routers are used to integrate service cores with backbone network. These routers have been used for nationwide all service operation (Core, Aggregation and Billing) of Nepal Telecom. ATN routers are used for access links. 

  • Under Contract No. NDCL/BTD/05/072-73 IP/MPLS Optical Transmission Equipment for Spur Network, signed with Honey Enterprises Pvt. Ltd., Nepal, dated 24 March 2017; 66 units of CX600-X2-M8A routers are purchased. They are equipped with 100/1000M(10 ports) and 10G (2 ports) interface cards and  240G Network Processing Cards (NPU).
  • Under Contract No. NDCL/BTD/05/075-76 IPRAN Equipment, signed with China Communications Services International Ltd, Hong Kong, dated 12 May 2019; two types of routers are purchased.
  1. 55 units of CX600-X2-M16A routers are purchased. They are equipped with 100/1000M (10 ports) and 10G (10 ports) interface cards and 1T Network Processing Cards (NPU).
  2. 320 units of ATN910C-F routers are purchased.
  • Under the additional order contract to NDCL/BTD/05/075-76 IPRAN Equipment, dated 4 April 2022; 100G cards are purchased and some of Cx600 routers are upgraded to 100G interface.

With the purchase of 100G interface cards, Nepal Telecom has undertaken version upgrade of routers and swapping of interface cards and NPU cards regardless of whether the router is under IP/MPLS or IPRAN. 

  • 100/1000M (10 ports) cards are not commonly used. It is gradually replaced by 10G (2 port card).
  • 10G (10 Ports) cards are very commonly used. 10G (2 Ports) cards are replaced by 10G (10 Ports) cards at most of sites where traffic demand is increasing.
  • 100G (1 port ) cards are used to upgrade uplinks at high traffic major sites
  • Most of routers have under gone version upgrade to latest Version 8.200 as recommended by the Supplier. 

 

 

 

Upgrading MNS to NCE

In response to letter, dated March 2, 2023, from BTD to CCSI regarding Quotation for Spares under contract no NDCL/BTD/ICB/02/075-76 for IPRAN Equipment, CCSI mentions, letter dated March 24, 2023, that new line cards need following changes:

  • The device needs to upgrade to R21 version and add on patch files
  • Exiting NMS U2000 will not be able to manage new cards. The devices need to integrate to NCE.
